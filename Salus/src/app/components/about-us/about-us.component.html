About us

Salus is an online platform designed to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle. With Salus, you can:

Browse a variety of nutritious recipes

Find recipes that cater to your dietary needs and preferences

Access detailed instructions and nutritional information for each recipe, including the calorie count

Track your daily calorie intake to help you stay on track and avoid overeating

Recipes

Salus offers a wide range of healthy recipes that cater to various dietary needs and preferences. Whether you're vegan, gluten-free, or looking for low-carb options, Salus has something for everyone.

Each recipe comes with detailed instructions and nutritional information, including the calorie count, so you can make informed decisions about your food choices.

Calorie Tracker

Salus also provides a calorie tracker feature that allows you to keep track of your daily calorie intake. This tool is especially useful for those looking to lose weight or maintain a healthy weight, as it helps you stay on track and avoid overeating.

With Salus, you can take control of your health and well-being by making healthier food choices and keeping track of your calorie intake. Whether you're a seasoned health enthusiast or just starting on your wellness journey, Salus is the perfect platform to help you achieve your goals.
